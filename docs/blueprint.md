# **App Name**: Eternal Kiss

## Core Features:

- Image Upload: Allow users to upload two individual images or one image containing two people.
- AI Video Transformation: Use the Runaway API to transform the uploaded images into a short video of the two people kissing realistically.
- Progress Display: Display a progress bar during video generation.
- Video Playback: Play the completed video upon successful generation.
- Whop Paywall: Implement a paywall that requires users to pay €7.50 for 15 days of access to view the generated video, payment via Whop.

## Style Guidelines:

- Light sky blue (#A7D9ED), reflecting a serene and romantic aesthetic.
- Off-white (#FAFAFA), providing a clean and unobtrusive backdrop to ensure clear visibility of the images and generated content.
- Muted violet (#C0B4DE), used for interactive elements to subtly draw the user's eye and enhance the user interface.
- Inter', a sans-serif font offering clean, modern typography suitable for a professional and accessible feel.
- Clean, white background to focus on the uploaded images and video content.
- Image upload section: Light mode style with soft borders, clear upload prompts, and distinct 'Single Image' and 'Two Images' buttons.
